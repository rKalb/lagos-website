Lagos Cloud is a managed transcription service built and operated by the Lagos team. It provides cloud-grade accuracy without requiring you to sign up for a third-party API.

## What It Will Offer

- **Sub-1% word error rate** — on par with the best commercial speech APIs
- **No API key management** — subscribe and it just works
- **AI cleanup included** — automatic filler word removal and grammar polish, no extra key needed
- **Priority support** — faster response times for subscribers

## Pricing

## Privacy

When using Lagos Cloud:
- Audio is sent to Lagos servers over an encrypted connection (TLS)
- Audio is used for transcription only and **immediately deleted** after processing
- No audio is stored or retained
- Transcription results are returned to your device and stored locally in your Lagos history

See our [Privacy Policy](/privacy) for full details.

## Difference from BYOK

Lagos Cloud vs. BYOK (your own key):
- **Lagos Cloud**: Single subscription, no key management, AI cleanup included, managed infrastructure
- **BYOK**: You pay your own provider directly, full control, varies by provider

For users who want simplicity, Lagos Cloud is the right choice. For users who want maximum control or cost optimization at high volume, BYOK may be preferable.
